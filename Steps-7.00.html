Introduction 7

In this project, you will learn how to create a Power BI dashboard from scratch for Bongoland. Bongoland Bike & Accessories is a global cycling supply retailing company headquartered in OH. This giant retail has combined annual sales of approximately $25 billion and employs about 38,000 associates globally.

You will create a dashboard and highlight 3 key KPIs - Total Revenue, Year over Year (YoY), and Gross Profit Margin (GPM%).

To create this dashboard, you will import 8 CSV files related to customers, products, regions, orders, and sales details into the Power BI system. Using this data, you will create different kinds of visualizations to represent various aspects of the imported data.
