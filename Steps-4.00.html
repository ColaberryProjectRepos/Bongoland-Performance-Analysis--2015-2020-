Executive Summary 4

Summary

Bongoland Bikes & Accessories is a global cycling supply retailing company headquartered in OH. This giant retail has combined annual sales of approximately $25 billion and employs about 38,000 associates globally.

Bongoland store is your resource for quality bicycles, gear, and bike services, they are always ready to help everyone from beginners to advanced cyclists.

This analysis focuses on three KPIs - Revenue Per Month, Revenue Year over Year, and Company's Gross Profit Margin to provide analysis and perspective on the way business is trending,

and identify changes in revenue that occurred in different sales territories under which the company operates.
