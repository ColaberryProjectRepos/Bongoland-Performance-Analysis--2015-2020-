Creating DAX Measures 11

In this step, I will create measures

To Create a DAX Measure follow these steps:

Step 1 - Select the table

Step 2 - Right Click and select New Measure

Step 3 - Write the following DAX Measures on the space provide one at a time and click the right mark

Total Costs = SUMX('BS_Sales','BS_Sales'[Quantity_Sold] *RELATED('BS_Products'[Unit Cost


Total Revenue = SUMX('BS_Sales','BS_Sales'[Quantity_Sold] *RELATED('BS_Products'[Unit Price]))


Total Revenue LM = CALCULATE([Total Revenue],DATEADD('Calender'[Date],-1,MONTH))


Total Sales = SUMX('BS_Sales','BS_Sales'[Quantity_Sold] *RELATED('BS_Products'[Unit Price]))

Total Revenue LY = CALCULATE([Total Revenue],DATEADD('Calender'[Date],-1,YEAR))


Revenue Per Month = DIVIDE([Total Revenue],DISTINCTCOUNT(Calender[YearMonthShort]),0)+0


YOY% = DIVIDE([Total Revenue]-[Total Revenue LY],[Total Revenue LY],0) change the format to percentage


GPM = DIVIDE([Total Profit],[Total Revenue],0)


Arrow YOY% = SWITCH(TRUE(), [YOY%] <0, UNICHAR(9660), UNICHAR(9650))
